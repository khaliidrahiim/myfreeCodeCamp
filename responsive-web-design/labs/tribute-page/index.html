Hamilton Lavity Stoutt

Hamilton Lavity Stoutt (7 November 1929 – 14 May 1995) was a British Virgin Islands politician who served as the first Chief Minister of the British Virgin Islands from 1967 to 1971 and again from 1979 to 1983. He was also the longest-serving elected representative in the territory's history, having been elected to the Legislative Council (now the House of Assembly) for a record eight terms.
