Rest in Peace Eileene L. Parsons OBE

🕊️ ✨Today, we honour the memory of the remarkable Eileene L. Parsons OBE, a visionary leader and champion of education, as well as a significant figure in the Virgin Islands Emancipation Festival history.

Her invaluable contributions to the Virgin Islands Emancipation Festival will never be forgotten. Her dedication and passion played a pivotal role in preserving the rich cultural heritage and traditions of the islands, ensuring that the Virgin Islands Emancipation Festival remained a cherished and vibrant celebration of freedom and resilience.

Eileene's tireless commitment to promoting awareness and understanding of the history behind festival has left an indelible mark on the community, inspiring generations to honour their roots and celebrate the spirit of unity and empowerment. Her legacy lives on through her enduring legacy, both in education and festival heritage and will continue to live in the hearts of all who had the privilege of knowing her.

Rest in peace, Eileene, your legacy lives on! 🕊️

#RememberingEileeneParsons #EducationAdvocate #VirginIslandsFestivalLegacy
